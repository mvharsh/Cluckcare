✅Happy & Healthy Birdie!✅

Want to know your feathered friend is feeling fantastic? Look for these signs:


  • 🐣 Shiny, clean feathers: They preen themselves regularly, so their feathers should look bright and well-kept.
  • 🐣 Sparkling eyes: Clear, bright eyes are a good sign your chicken is feeling alert and healthy.
  • 🐣 Full of energy: Happy chicken love to chirp and explore their surroundings.
  • 🐣 Healthy appetite: Chicken with a good appetite gobble up their food with enthusiasm.
  • 🐣 Regular droppings: Normal poop is a good indicator of a healthy digestive system.

    ✅Tips To Keep Them Happy & Safe✅


  • 🐣 Delicious, balanced diet: Provide a variety of foods with essential nutrients to keep them energized.
  • 🐣 Fresh, clean water: Just like us, chicken need clean water to stay hydrated.
  • 🐣 Vet check-ups: Regular vet visits ensure your bird gets the care they need to stay healthy.
  • 🐣 Clean home: Keep their cage (or living area) clean to prevent any unwanted illnesses.
