🐥CLUCK CARE🐣


🐔🐥🐓 CluckCare is a website harnessing the power of deep learning convolutional neural networks (CNN-VGG) to predict chicken diseases from uploaded images of their excretions. Our simple yet effective approach aims to assist poultry farmers and veterinarians in promptly identifying potential health issues🐔🐥🐓.

🐥The reason behind creating CluckCare🐥


Raising chickens 🐔 is not just about producing eggs 🥚 or meat; it's about ensuring their health and well-being 🐥. However, identifying diseases 🦠 in chickens can be challenging, especially for novice farmers 🌾. With CluckCare, we aim to bridge this gap 🌉 by leveraging the power of deep learning convolutional neural networks (CNN-VGG) 🧠. By analyzing images of chicken excretions 📸, our platform can swiftly detect potential health issues 🚨, empowering farmers and veterinarians alike 🌱. Early detection means prompt intervention, ultimately safeguarding the health and productivity of poultry flocks 🐥👩‍⚕️. CluckCare strives to be a reliable ally in the poultry farming community 🐔💻.
