🚨Uh Oh! Watch Out for Cocci!🚨


Ever heard of a tiny critter called coccidia? It's a parasite that can give your chicken a hard time. These little guys live in a chicken's intestines and can cause some not-so-fun problems.


Signs your chicken might have cocci:


  • 🐣 Poopy problems: Look for watery droppings, which can be a sign of diarrhea.
  • 🐣 Shrinking chickie: Weight loss isn't good, so keep an eye on your chicken's weight.
  • 🐣 Puffy and grumpy? A fluffed-up appearance can be a sign of discomfort.
  • 🐣 Bloody mess: In severe cases, blood might be present in the droppings. Yikes!

    • Don't worry, we can help prevent cocci! Here's how to keep your chicken healthy:


  • 🐣 Cleanliness is key: Regularly clean and disinfect their coop to keep those parasites away.
  • 🐣 Superfood power: Give your chicken a balanced diet with all the good stuff to boost their immune system.
  • 🐣 Isolating the icky: If a chicken gets sick, separate them to stop the cocci from spreading to their friends.
